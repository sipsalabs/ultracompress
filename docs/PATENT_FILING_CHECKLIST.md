## Filing Steps
1. Go to https://patentcenter.uspto.gov
2. Select "File a new patent application"
3. Select "Provisional Application"
4. Upload PATENT_DRAFT.md as the specification (convert to PDF first)
5. Fill in inventor info: Sip (full legal name, address, email)
6. Select micro-entity status ($80 fee)
7. Pay $80 via credit/debit card
8. Save the confirmation number and filing receipt
