INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- A small range across roles means the Claim 21 savings mechanism
  is uniformly applicable to every transformer linear, not a
  role-specific trick (e.g. not unique to attention or to MLP).
- A large range would indicate the effect is concentrated in a
  subset of roles; the split should then be reported by role.
