Interpretation:
  concat-split > 0  => 3-stream split is STRICTLY BETTER for this coder
                       (streams have different byte distributions;
                        concatenation dilutes each distribution's
                        memoryless entropy by Jensen's inequality).
  concat-split < 0  => a single coder captures cross-stream structure
                       the 3-stream split was leaving on the table.
