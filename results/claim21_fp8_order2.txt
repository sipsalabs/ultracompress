INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- order-2 H is the tight lower bound for any coder that
  conditions on 2 previous bytes (65536 contexts x 256
  symbols = 16.8M states).
- 'br - H2' is how far brotli-11 sits above that floor.
  Still negative => brotli-11 is using order >= 3 context.
  Near-zero or positive => brotli-11 is essentially
  capturing all context-2 and no more.
- H1 -> H2 marginal gain quantifies how much additional
  savings a context-2 coder gets over a context-1 coder.
