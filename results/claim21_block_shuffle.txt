Claim-21 block-shuffle: characteristic context scale per stream
==================================================================================
Shuffle each stream in blocks of size B, then compress.
B=1   : full byte permutation (order-0 only).
B=full: as-emitted.
The block size at which savings saturate to within codec noise
of as-emitted is the characteristic context scale for that coder/stream.
