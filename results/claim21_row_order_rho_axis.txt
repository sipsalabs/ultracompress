KEY FINDING: fp8 + scale invariance holds AT EVERY RHO.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  fp8 shuf-sort %    : should be < 0.05% at every rho (per-row local byte
                       distribution, independent of how rows are sequenced).
  scale shuf-sort %  : should be < 1% at every rho.
  idx shuf-sort %    : monotone-decreasing with rho because higher rho means
                       more rows per linear -> sorted delta sequence becomes
                       MORE structured (smaller average delta) -> larger gap
                       vs shuffle. Claim 21 always emits sorted -> zero cost.
