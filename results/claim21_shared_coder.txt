INTERPRETATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- global worst delta bpB near 0 => a SINGLE static entropy
  coder built on any one model is near-optimal on every model
  for this stream. This is the operational form of the wave-22
  distribution-shape universality result.
- Large delta on scale confirms the wave-22 finding that scale
  distributions are model-specific and need per-model tables.
