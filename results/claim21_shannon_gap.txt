KEY OBSERVATION
---------------
- The idx_delta stream is coded BELOW its order-0 Shannon bound by
  every entropy coder we test: proves the delta sequence has real
  higher-order structure that shuffling destroys.
- fp8 and scale sit very near their order-0 bounds for brotli-11
  and lzma-6: those streams are close to memoryless, and the
  savings we observe are the intrinsic entropy deficit of the
  restored-row payload relative to uniform random bytes.
- lz4-hc sits ~1.35 bpb ABOVE the Shannon bound in aggregate,
  i.e. essentially no entropy coding - which is exactly why it's
  used as the negative control.
