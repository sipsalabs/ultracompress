Claim-21 varint/rice idx_delta emitter measurement
======================================================================================

Wave 23 predicted that a simple bit-level variable-length
integer encoder would close most of the 1.55 bpB gap between
observed brotli-11 idx_delta rate (4.34 bpB) and the Shannon
order-0 floor (2.78 bpB). This wave measures the gap directly.

All sizes reported in 'bpB(4-byte ref)' = total bits divided
by the int32 raw size (so 8.0 = no compression; 2.78 = floor).

INTERPRETATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- If LEB128 / Rice-best bpB is below 4.339 bpB, a bit-level coder
  SHIPS savings beyond brotli-11 on idx_delta. The margin is the
  direct empirical validation of the wave-23 prediction.
- If Rice-best is near the Shannon floor, the distribution is well
  modelled by a geometric distribution, confirming wave 21's
  picture of small-positive-integer deltas.
